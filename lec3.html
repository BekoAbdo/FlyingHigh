Directions & Places in the City

Asking for and giving directions | Common places vocabulary

Common Places in the City

1. Hospital: A place where sick people go to get treatment.
2. Men's toilet: A bathroom for men.
3. Women's toilet: A bathroom for women.
4. Bank: A place where we keep, save, or take money.
5. School: A place where children and students go to learn.
6. Construction site: A place where workers build things, like buildings or roads.
7. Bridge: A structure over a river or road to help people or cars cross.
8. Parking: A place for cars.
9. Library: A quiet place where you can read or borrow books.
10. Fire station: A place where fire trucks and firefighters stay.
11. Police station: A place where police officers work.
12. Bus station: A place where buses stop and people can get on or off.
13. Train station: A place where trains stop to take passengers.
14. Supermarket: A big store that sells food and many other things.
15. Grocery store: A small shop that sells food like bread, milk, and vegetables.
16. Pharmacy: A shop where you can buy medicine.
17. Market: A large shopping center with many stores.
18. Restaurant: A place where you buy and eat food.
19. Café: A small place to drink tea, coffee, or eat snacks.
20. Mosque: A place where Muslims pray.
21. Church: A place where Christians pray.
22. Airport: A place where airplanes land and take off.
23. Hotel: A place where you pay to sleep and stay.
24. Cinema: A place where you watch movies.
25. Theater: A place to watch live shows or plays.
26. Zoo: A place where you can see animals.
27. Museum: A place where you can see old or special things.
28. Playground: A place where children play.
29. Stadium: A big place to watch sports.
30. Post office: A place to send or receive letters and packages.
31. University: A big school for adults after high school.
32. College: A smaller school for learning after high school.
33. Apartment building: A building with many homes for people.
34. Factory: A place where people make things.
35. KTC: A place where you can learn english smoothly.
36. Gas station: A place where you put fuel in your car.
37. Barber shop: A place where men get haircuts.
38. Hair salon: A place where women get their hair done.
39. Clinic: A small health center to see a doctor.
40. Embassy: The official building of a foreign country.
41. Courthouse: A place where legal cases are decided.
42. City hall: A government building in a city.
43. Bakery: A shop that sells bread and cakes.
44. Butcher shop: A shop that sells fresh meat.
45. Flower shop: A place where you can buy flowers.
46. Bookstore: A shop that sells books.
47. Ice cream shop: A place where you can buy ice cream.
48. Swimming pool: A place where you swim.
49. Gym: A place to do exercises and keep fit.
50. Warehouse: A big building where things are stored.
51. Car wash: A place where cars are cleaned.
52. Picking area: A place where people collect things like fruits or vegetables (or in a store/warehouse: where workers pick items to pack), or where you can pick someone.

Asking for Directions

Excuse me, where is the [place]?
"Excuse me, where is the bank?"
How can I get to [place]?
"How can I get to the train station?"
Is there a [place] near here?
"Is there a café near here?"

Practice Dialogues

Dialogue 1:
A: "Excuse me, is there a supermarket near here?"
B: "Yes, go straight ahead and turn right at the bank. It's on your left."
A: "Thank you very much!"
B: "You're welcome."
Dialogue 2:
A: "How can I get to the train station?"
B: "Walk two blocks, then turn left. It's opposite the park."
A: "Is it far?"
B: "No, about 5 minutes walk."
